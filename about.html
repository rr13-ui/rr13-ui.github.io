Life Expectancy Analysis

Life Expectancy for Female Life Expectancy For Men About

About:Life expectancy factors can be analyzed by many factors. Have picked up couple of factors, to show the differeces. This project is based on hybrid interactive slide show. Each slide has similar template, fonts, bar graphs and tooltip to provide visual consistency on scene. Slides are in order to convey the message each slide has. For consistent annotations each slide has details and trends analysis in similar format. X and y Axis has been labeled clearly.

Parameters and triggers has been used in all slides. Upon mouseover any bar it changes its color to Orange and displays the value.When you moveout your mouse, color turns back to blue. Hyperlinks and pagination will let the user move to different slides.
